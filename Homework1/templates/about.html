Meet The Founder

Duong Ngoc Linh Dan

Hello! I am Linh Dan, the founder and head baker of Sweetie Bakery. Baking has been my passion since I was a little girl watching my grandmother in the kitchen.

I founded Sweetie Bakery with a simple mission: "To bring happiness through the sweetness of natural ingredients."

Every cake you eat here is made with my personal recipe, ensuring not only delicious taste but also healthiness for you and your family.
